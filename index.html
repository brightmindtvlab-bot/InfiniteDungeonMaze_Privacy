Privacy Policy — Infinite Dungeon Maze

Last updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Stardust Colony ("the game") handles information when you play.

Information We Collect

Stardust Colony does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to play.

Game Save Data

Your game progress is saved locally on your device and optionally backed up to Firebase (a Google service) to protect your progress if you reinstall the app or switch devices. This save data contains only game progress information such as your colony level, crops, and achievements. It does not contain your name, email address, location, or any other personal information.

Third Party Services

The game uses Firebase by Google for cloud save functionality. Firebase may collect anonymous usage and crash data according to Google's own privacy policy, which can be found at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

Stardust Colony is suitable for all ages. The game does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Advertising

Stardust Colony contains no advertisements.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date at the top.
